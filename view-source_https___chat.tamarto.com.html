How Tamarto Works

Three simple steps to absolute communication security

1

Compose Your Message

Type your message in the Tamarto interface. The app immediately begins encrypting with Argon2id + AES-256-GCM and sharding your content directly in your browser.

2

Secure Sharding & Transmission

Your message is split into encrypted shards using sophisticated browser cryptography. Shards are transmitted to separate nodes with no history storage.

3

Receive & Delete Anytime

Recipient's browser reassembles shards into the original message using Argon2id-derived keys. Any user can delete communications at any time, permanently erasing them.
